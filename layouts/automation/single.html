Automation

This is our favourite part! Document automation is a simple recipe: build a form, code a document template and mix them together. But like any recipe, a good finished product depends on bringing quality ingredients together in just the right way...

Automation services

We can help you with as much or as little of the document automation process as you need. We specialise in automating legal documents for law-firms, in-house legal teams and other document-dependent businesses.

Building forms

Building a good form is a balancing act. It must be precise and accurate, but also simple and user friendly (or else why bother?). Guidance should available, but not suffocating. A good form should also look good! We can do it all.

Coding document templates

Your well drafted precedent might make perfect sense to a person, but unfortunately can't speak to your form without a translator. We add the code necessary to get your content and your form on the same page (literally).

Testing

Test. Test. Test. Not a glamorous step, but an essential one. Every variable, every field, every piece of conditional logic must be tested again. And again. That's how we ensure that our automated documents are robust, reliable and predictable.

Setting delivery options

Who should have access to which form? How will the document be delivered? In which formats? Who should have access to the records? We'll guide you through the options to tailor the solution to you.
