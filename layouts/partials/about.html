We love document automation. Why? It's so much more than just creating documents quickly. It takes invaluable knowledge locked up in people's heads and makes it accessible to a wider audience. It promotes a degree of standardisation which, rather than stifle creativity, allows talented authors to focus on what really matters (rather than moving punctuation around a page). It enables continuous improvement by turning documents into projects that are improved over time, rather than forgotten.

We want to see law firms, in-house legal departments and other document-dependent businesses (and their clients and customers) start getting access to these benefits now and stop missing out because of the time, effort and funds necessary to develop a whole new skill-set internally. That's why our focus is on making implementing high-quality document automation projects easy and accessible to everyone.

Another thing we love about document automation is the collision between designing good user experience (UX) and the precision necessary to navigate complex concepts (especially legal concepts) - that's probably because our founder was a top-tier practicing lawyer and will remain a legal nerd for life!
